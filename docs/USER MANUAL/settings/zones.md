A zone is a designated area in your app or site where personalized content is delivered from Redfast. Zones are designed to blend in with the overall experience of your service and deliver subtle guidance to the end user.

You can deliver text-only content or a display item into your zone. You can also deliver invisible actions using zones. For example, showing fewer ads to a VIP or first time user.
