# Definition

<div class="rp-definition">
  Promotions are marketing strategies that increase customer engagement and drive sales. Recurly supports four promotion types: free trials, coupons and discounts, gift subscriptions, and gift cards — each configurable from the Admin Console or via API.
</div>

# Key details

This page is an overview of Recurly's promotion tools. Select a card below to go to the full documentation for each feature.

<div class="rp-nav-grid">
  <Cards cols={2}>
    <Card title="Free trial management" href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/plans#section-free-trials" target="_blank">
      Offer potential customers a free trial period to experience your product before committing to a paid subscription. Trials are configured at the plan level and automatically convert to a paid subscription when the period ends.
    </Card>
    <Card title="Coupons and discounts" href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/coupons" target="_blank">
      Apply percentage or fixed-amount discounts to subscriptions or one-time charges. Recurly supports bulk unique coupon generation and multiple coupons per account.
    </Card>
    <Card title="Gift subscriptions" href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/gift-subscriptions" target="_blank">
      Let existing customers purchase a subscription as a gift for someone else — a natural driver of brand awareness and new customer acquisition.
    </Card>
    <Card title="Gift cards" href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/gift-cards" target="_blank">
      Issue prepaid stored-value cards that customers can redeem as payment for subscriptions or one-time purchases. Available on Pro and Elite plans.
    </Card>
    <Card title="Gift cards dashboard" href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/gift-cards-dashboard" target="_blank">
      Manage the creation, issuance, and status of gift cards from a single dashboard. Available on Pro and Elite plans.
    </Card>
  </Cards>
</div>
