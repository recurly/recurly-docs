## Support

Recurly.js supports Chrome, Firefox, Safari, iOS, Edge, and IE 11+.

For Recurly related questions or previous versions of Recurly.js documentation, please contact [support@recurly.com](https://support.recurly.com/) for help with your account or other general questions.
