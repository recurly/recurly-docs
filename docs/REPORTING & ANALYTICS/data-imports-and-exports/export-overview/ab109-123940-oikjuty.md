# Overview

### Required plan

This export is only available to merchants that are using Recurly’s <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/vindicia-retain-integration" target="_blank">Vindicia Retain integration</a>.

### Prerequisites

To enable and activate the External Recoveries export, your Recurly account must have the <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/vindicia-retain-integration" target="_blank">Vindicia Retain integration</a> enabled.

# Definition

The External Recoveries export provides detailed analysis on what is happening or has happened to the past due invoices that will be or have been sent to Vindicia Retain for collection through Recurly’s Vindicia Retain integration.  Click <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/vindicia-retain-integration" target="_blank">here</a> to learn more about Recurly’s Vindicia Retain integration.

## Invoice status

* **Scheduled**: Scheduled: Scheduled to be sent to Vindicia.
* **Processing:** Vindicia is currently attempting collections.
* **Success:** Vindicia was able to collect payment.
* **Declined:** Vindicia failed to collect the payment.
* **Canceled:** The invoice was sent to Vindicia, but then Recurly asked Vindicia to stop collection attempts (e.g. in the case Recurly was able to collect through a dunning action)
* **Failed:** Recurly was unable to send the invoice to Vindicia due to errors.

# Key benefits

* **View scheduled invoices**: See which invoices are scheduled to be sent to Vindicia Retain for collection.
* **Track invoices in collection**: View which invoices are currently with Vindicia Retain for collection.
* **Check collection outcomes**: Determine which invoices Vindicia Retain was or wasn’t able to collect.

# Filters

#### Time Range

Filters for all invoices that are scheduled to be sent to Vindicia (i.e. past due invoices) or invoices that have been sent to Vindicia that have a posted/creation date that match your date criteria
