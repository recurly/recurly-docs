# Definition

<div class="rp-definition">Payment gateways are the secure conduits that carry payment data from your customer to the payment processor and back. Recurly supports a wide selection of reputable gateways — each one configurable directly from your dashboard — so you can match your payment infrastructure to your operational needs, regional requirements, and preferred payment methods.</div>
