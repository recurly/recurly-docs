# Definition

Recurly's Hosted Pages offer a set of out-of-the-box solutions, designed for quick deployment with minimal development intervention. With tools like [Checkout ](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/checkout)(**new**), Hosted Payment Pages and Hosted Account Management, businesses can provide their customers with seamless subscription sign-up experiences, purchase capabilities, and account management portals.

### Dates and Times

Recurly records all transactions in **UTC** as the underlying billing timezone. Any dates or times shown on Hosted Pages are automatically converted from UTC to the local time specified in your site settings.

# Key benefits

* **Rapid deployment:** Kickstart subscription processes without waiting for development resources.
* **Diverse payment options:** Support for 3D Secure, credit cards, bank accounts, PayPal, and Amazon.
* **Efficient management:** Offer subscribers a comprehensive portal to view invoices, credits, manage billing details, and more.
* **Heightened security:** Abide by high PCI compliance standards, ensuring the utmost security for payment processes.
* **Localization:** Multi-lingual support catering to a global customer base.

# Key Details

## Checkout

Recurly [Checkout ](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/checkout)provides a fluid checkout experience that caters to the varying needs of your customers. It offers merchants the flexibility to balance between using a comprehensive API/RJS integration and a straightforward Hosted Payment page, enhancing both customer and developer experiences within a secure, PCI-compliant environment.

## Hosted Payment Pages

Recurly's [Hosted Payment Pages](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/hosted-payment-pages) offer a specialized platform that simplifies the initiation of user subscriptions. Along with Account Management Pages, these platforms create a comprehensive and engaging user experience within a secure, PCI-compliant environment.

## Hosted Account Management

Recurly's [Hosted Account Management](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/hosted-account-management) portal exemplifies efficient subscription management. It allows existing subscribers to access detailed information about their subscriptions, such as active plans, past invoices, and account credits. Businesses can customize the portal experience, switching between a "Guest View" for access only through that link and "Account Login" for a secure login system for full account access within a secure, PCI-compliant environment.
