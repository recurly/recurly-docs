# Definition

<div class="rp-definition">A transaction in Recurly represents a single payment exchange between you and a subscriber — the moment a financial interaction with your product or service is recorded, processed, and logged. Transactions span every payment method and medium Recurly supports, giving you a complete, auditable record of your revenue activity.</div>
