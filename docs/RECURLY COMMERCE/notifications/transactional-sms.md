# 📱 Enabling transactional SMS

<br />

Recurly Commercerecommends toggling on no more than 3 SMS notifications for subscribers. (If a subscriber opts out of the SMS notifications they cannot opt back in unless the phone number is updated on their subscription contract.)

<br />

The following 6 comms are available for SMS:

1. **Subscription created:** Confirms that a subscription order has been created
2. **Order placed:** Confirms that an order has been placed
3. **Failed payment method:** Notifies customers when their order could not be placed due to payment failure *(highly recommended)*
4. **Subscription cancelled:** Confirms that a subscription contract has been cancelled
5. **Product out of stock:** Notifies customers when one or more products they are subscribed to are out of stock *(highly recommended)*
6. **Upcoming order:** Notifies customers 3 days before their subscription order is due to renew *(highly recommended)*
