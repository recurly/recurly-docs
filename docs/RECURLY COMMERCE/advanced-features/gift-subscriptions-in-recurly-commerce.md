## Definition

A **Gift Subscription** is a subscription that one customer purchases as a gift, allowing the recipient to redeem, manage, and personalize their subscription independently.

## Key benefits

* **Increase customer acquisition**: Turn subscribers into brand ambassadors as they gift subscriptions to friends and family.
* **Enhance engagement**: Recipients experience the same seamless subscription journey, driving long-term retention.
* **Boost average order value**: Offering gifting options encourages higher cart values and repeat purchases.

## Key details

### Enabling the gift option

1. Navigate to **Subscription Plans** in the Recurly Commerce app.
2. When **creating** or **editing** an offer, scroll to the **Subscription Options** section.
3. Toggle **Add Gift Option** on to enable the “Is this a gift?” prompt in your subscription widget.
4. Click **Confirm** (or **Save**) to apply your changes.

> **Note**: Only products included in the offer will display the gift prompt.
