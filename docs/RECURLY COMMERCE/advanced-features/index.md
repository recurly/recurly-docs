# Overview

Advanced features in Recurly Commerce unlock new levels of automation, gifting flexibility, and bulk operations to streamline your workflows and delight subscribers at scale.

# Key benefits

* **Automate key touchpoints**: Trigger trial swaps, add-ons, and more without manual intervention.
* **Delight with gifting**: Let customers send and manage gift subscriptions end-to-end.
* **Scale with bulk updates**: Modify hundreds or thousands of subscribers in one go.

# Key details

* **Automations**: Configure event-driven workflows—like free trials, product swaps, and add-ons—to surprise and engage subscribers. Learn more <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/automations#/" target="_blank">here</a>.
* **Gift Subscriptions**: Enable your “Add Gift Option” toggle when creating or editing offers to launch a full gifting experience for givers and recipients. Learn more <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/gift-subscriptions-in-recurly-commerce#/" target="_blank">here</a>.
* **Bulk Updater**: Perform mass edits to delivery dates, frequencies, statuses, and more across your subscriber base—ideal for plan migrations or large-scale adjustments. Learn more <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/bulk-updater-in-recurly-commerce#/" target="_blank">here</a>.
