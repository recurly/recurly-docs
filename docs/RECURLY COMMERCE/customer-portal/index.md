# Overview

The Customer Portal is the self-service area where subscribers can view and manage every aspect of their subscriptions—skips, pauses, swaps, gifting, billing info, and more.

# Key benefits

* **Empower subscribers**: Give customers the controls they need to adjust orders, pause deliveries, or update shipping without support tickets.
* **Reduce support load**: Automate routine account changes, freeing your team to focus on exceptions.
* **Boost retention**: Offering flexible self-service options helps prevent unnecessary cancellations.
