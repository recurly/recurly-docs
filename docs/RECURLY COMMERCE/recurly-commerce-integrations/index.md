# Overview

Recurly Commerce integrations let you extend your subscription workflows by connecting to third-party tools—whether for messaging, support, or automation—directly within the Recurly Commerce dashboard.

# Key benefits

* **Streamlined setup**: Browse and enable integrations without custom development.
* **Unified data flow**: Keep subscriber and order data in sync across your favorite platforms.
* **Enhanced subscriber experience**: Leverage specialized tools (SMS, email, helpdesk) to engage and support customers at every stage.

# Key details

* **Email & SMS Integrations**: Connect messaging platforms like Klaviyo, Postscript, and Attentive to automate transactional comms and marketing campaigns.\
  Learn more: <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/email-and-sms-integration#/" target="_blank">Email & SMS integration</a>

* **Gorgias Integration**: Surface subscriber profiles and subscription controls directly in your Gorgias helpdesk for faster, more informed support.\
  Learn more: <a href="https://docs.recurly.com/docs/gorgias-integration#/" target="_blank">Gorgias integration</a>
