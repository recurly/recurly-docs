# Definition

The **storefront widget** is the on-page component shoppers use to subscribe. This guide shows you how to tailor its text, layout, and styling so it seamlessly aligns with your store’s look and feel.

# Key benefits

* **Brand consistency**: Match fonts, colors, and messaging to your storefront theme.
* **Enhanced user experience**: Drag-and-drop purchase options to surface your most important offers.
* **Rapid iteration**: Preview and publish style updates instantly—no developer needed.

# Key details

## Edit and stylize storefront components

Once the widget is installed, you can customize every aspect of its design in the Theme Editor. Be sure to click **Save and set live** after making changes.

1. **Open** the Shopify Theme Editor for your live theme.
2. **Select** the Recurly Commerce widget section on your product template.
3. Customize:
   * **Text labels** (e.g. change “Subscribe” to “Become a Member”).
   * **Option order** by dragging purchase and one-time blocks.
   * **Style details** like colors, borders, and typography.
4. **Save and set live** to publish your new design.Still need help? Contact <a href="mailto:support@recurly.com" target="_blank">[support@recurly.com](mailto:support@recurly.com)</a>.
