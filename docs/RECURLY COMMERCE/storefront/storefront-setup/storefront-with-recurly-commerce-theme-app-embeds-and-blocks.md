This guide walks you through how to **add**, **remove** and **customize** the Prive Subscriptions app embeds and app blocks that will power your Prive **Subscriptions storefront experience** directly from your [Shopify Theme Editor](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-store/themes/customizing-themes/edit). Once you are finished with the steps below, your storefront will be configured so that shoppers can purchase and manage their subscriptions.

# The Mandatory App Embed Block

💡 Whether you have a vintage theme or an [Operating System 2.0 Theme](https://themes.shopify.com/collections/os2-themes) **you need to complete this step.**

For all merchants, the base Prive Subscriptions experience for the storefront is configured by enabling the **Prive Subscription App Embed Block**.

## Adding Embed Block

### **Step 1:** Open the theme editor

💡 You can complete the steps below from any page in the theme editor. If you want to preview the widget in action (as shown in the gif below), make sure that you have created a subscription offer, and that you navigate to a product page that contains a product with the subscription offer.

### **Step 2:** Add the Prive App embed block

To add the Prive app embed, go to the Shopify Theme Editor > Theme Settings > App embeds and toggle the **Prive subscriptions widget** on.

<Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/135c716f1242ad4b10f942180d079fa7d14a33499fec881e5af32223cf13b4bf-App_embeds.gif" />

### Step 3: Click Save & Go live 🎉

In the right-hand corner, click **Save**. You are ready to go. Shoppers will now be able to go to your store and purchase a subscription according to the Subscription Offers you configured in the Prive Subscriptions App.

![](https://files.readme.io/28777881cbee1ab15331985762f0aac8db0833a62a00fbc76d8c957a0b2cea2b-image.png)

## Removing Embed Block

Removing the embed block is as simple as toggling the **Prive subscriptions widget** off and clicking **Save** in the top right corner. Doing so will remove all Prive storefront components and will disable your shopper's ability to purchase or manage a subscription.

![](https://files.readme.io/71414dfec365a655df4b96a3a688dfe4d6621bb40c5a3160ed6cb2506b82d170-image.png)

<br />

## Customizing the Embed Block

We don’t currently offer customizations to our embed block, though this feature will be coming soon.

# Optional App Blocks

💡 Merchants utilizing [Operating System 2.0 Themes](https://themes.shopify.com/collections/os2-themes) will have additional capabilities through our inline app block. This block provides you with the ability to change the position of the widget on your product pages.

## Adding App Block

Below are 4 easy steps to add and publish the Recurly Commerce subscriptions app block.

![](https://files.readme.io/c7710c5592ecdd011e45e5d292167c2646d0f0d6fed0de27cc7bdd6d0237c2af-image.png)

<br />

### **Step 1:** Open themed editor

Before adding the app block make sure that you have the theme editor open and navigate to a product page that has a subscription offer attached to it.

### **Step 2: Add Block**

Expand the “Product information” section and click on “Add block”. Under the “APPS” section click on the “Prive subscriptions widget” app block.

### **Step 3:** Move Prive Subscriptions Widget block above the "Buy button"

### **Step 4:** Click Save & Go live 🎉

In the right-hand corner, click **Save**. You are ready to go. Shoppers will now be able to go to your store and purchase a subscription according to the Subscription Offers you configured in the Prive Subscriptions App.

## Removing App Block

To remove the app block, click on the “Prive subscriptions widget” app block and then click “Remove block” in the bottom left-hand corner and then click Save.

## Customizing the App Block

We don’t currently offer customizations to our app blocks. This feature will be coming soon.
