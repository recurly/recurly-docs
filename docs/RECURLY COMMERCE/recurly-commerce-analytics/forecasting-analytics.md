# Overview

Recurly Commerce Forecasting unlocks smarter revenue and subscription order predictions. With Recurly Commerce Forecasting, merchants can accurately predict future revenue, orders, and SKU sales for the next 12 months.

### Prerequisites & limitations

* Available on Pro and Enterprise plans only.

# Definition

Forecasting leverages your current active, paused, and dunning subscriptions to project future order volumes and gross sales across customizable time windows.

# Key benefits

* **Predict 12 months ahead**: See expected revenue, order counts, and SKU unit sales well into the future.
* **Optimize inventory & operations**: Align stock levels, staffing, and fulfillment with anticipated demand.
* **Drive smarter marketing**: Use forecasted AOV and order trends to fine-tune promotions and retention campaigns.

### Filter & display options

<br />

* **Time window:** Current month, next 3, 6, or 12 months
  * **Aggregation:** Daily or monthly
    * **Contract scope:** Active only; Active + Paused; Active + Paused + Dunning
      * **Metric type:** SKU units or revenue
        <br />
        > **Note:** Forecasts auto-refresh once per day. The refresh timestamp is shown on the dashboard; data is not updated in real time.
        >
        > <br />

### Metrics

<br />

**Total gross sales forecasted**\
Forecasted total gross sales from subscription orders for the selected period.

<br />

**Total subscription orders forecasted**\
Forecasted total number of subscription orders for the selected period.

<br />

**Forecasted average order value (AOV)**\
Forecasted average total of every subscription order for the selected period.
