# Overview

Product Breakdown Analytics is a customizable, tabular report that attributes subscription metrics (order count, units sold, and revenue) to individual product variants for both first-time and all subsequent subscription orders.

# Key benefits

* **Identify best-selling variants:** Quickly see which SKUs attract the most subscribers and generate the highest revenue.
* **Optimize inventory & pricing:** Use variant-level insights to adjust stock levels, pricing, and promotional strategies.
* **Measure subscriber behavior:** Compare first‐order versus all‐order performance to understand upsell and retention impact by variant.

# Key details

* **Metric filters:** Toggle between viewing **Order Count**, **Product Units**, or **Revenue**.
* **Order scope:** Choose to report on **First Subscription Orders** only, or **All Subscription Orders** across a subscriber’s lifetime.
* **Time granularity:** Switch your table’s timeframe to **Daily** or **Monthly** to analyze short-term trends or long-term performance.
* **Interactive sorting & filtering:** Use the header filters to focus on specific variants, time periods, or metrics for a tailored analysis.
