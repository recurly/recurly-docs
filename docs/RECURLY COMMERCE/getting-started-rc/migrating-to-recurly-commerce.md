# Overview

### Additional cost

A one-time migration fee applies. Please reach out to your Recurly account manager or <a href="mailto:support@recurly.com" target="_blank">[support@recurly.com](mailto:support@recurly.com)</a> for pricing details.

### Prerequisites & limitations

* Your store must be hosted on Shopify or Shopify Plus.
* Migration supports up to **100,000** subscribers per day.

# Definition

A Recurly Commerce migration is a fully automated process that pulls your existing subscription data—accounts, subscription terms, billing schedules, and customer profiles—from another platform into Recurly Commerce, with 99.9%+ data fidelity.

# Key benefits

* **Zero subscriber disruption**: Your customers experience no breaks in service or unexpected changes.
* **Rapid turnaround**: Complete migrations in days, not weeks—processing up to 100,000 subscribers per day.
* **Revenue protection and uplift**: Automated data validation often uncovers recoverable revenue and reduces churn during the migration.

# Key detail

## Migration process

1. **Install Recurly Commerce**

   * Download the Recurly Commerce app from the Shopify App Store.
   * Choose Core or Pro plan and complete checkout in Shopify.

2. **Grant collaborator access**

   * Accept the collaborator invitation from Recurly Commerce.
   * **Note:** Do not alter requested permissions—they ensure full migration and support.

3. **Kick-off call**

   * Book a 15-minute session with your Recurly Commerce CSM to scope and schedule the migration work.
   * Contact <a href="mailto:support@recurly.com" target="_blank">[support@recurly.com](mailto:support@recurly.com)</a> to arrange.

4. **Submit migration questionnaire**

   * Complete our [Migration Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeTDvctTNzJI3PBCi1miHTqYq7q3XYR1gcYGD6bSIlPRIxYEw/viewform) with your Shopify and Stripe API keys.
   * This enables us to pull in your subscriber contracts, plans, and customer profiles.

5. **Approve storefront components**

   * We’ll install the Recurly Commerce subscription widget and customer portal on your site.
   * Review and confirm before go-live. Your CSM will notify you once everything is active.

## During migration

Our engineers and your CSM handle the entire data transfer—no action required on your end. You’ll receive regular progress updates until completion.

## Post migration

* Celebrate! 🎉 Your data is now fully in Recurly Commerce.
* Book a final onboarding session with your CSM to review best practices, explore analytics, and optimize your new setup.
