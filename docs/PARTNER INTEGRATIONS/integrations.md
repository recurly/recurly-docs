# Integration partners

Below is a list of Recurly’s integration partners. Each has been identified for its compatibility with the Recurly subscription management system. These partners offer a range of tools and platforms to support various operational aspects when using Recurly.

## ISVs

**Braze**: Braze is a comprehensive customer engagement platform that facilitates meaningful customer experiences through its flexible and open ecosystem. It offers over 140 technology partnership integrations, enabling personalized messaging, orchestration, and data analytics among other capabilities. Integration involves discovery, planning, execution, quality assurance, and maintenance, with customization options to suit specific business needs.

**Avalara AvaTax Integration**: Avalara AvaTax provides automated tax compliance services. It integrates with various e-commerce and payment platforms to automatically calculate tax rates in real-time, based on geolocation and the latest tax laws.

**ChartMogul**: ChartMogul is a subscription data analytics platform that helps businesses measure, understand, and grow their subscription-based revenues. It integrates with various billing systems to aggregate financial metrics like MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), churn rate, and customer lifetime value.

**FlowEQ**: FlowEQ specializes in streamlining complex workflows into simple, user-friendly processes. This likely involves integrating with customer support and workflow management tools to enhance operational efficiency and customer service quality.

**Google Cloud Platform**: Google Cloud Platform offers a suite of cloud computing services that runs on the same infrastructure that Google uses internally for its end-user products. It provides a wide range of services including computing, data storage, data analytics, and machine learning, and integrates with various tools and applications to support scalable, secure, and flexible cloud solutions.

**Kount**: An advanced digital fraud prevention solution, Kount empowers businesses to detect and prevent online fraud in real-time, ensuring secure customer transactions.

**Mulesoft**: MuleSoft provides integration software for connecting applications, data, and devices. It offers an API-led approach to connectivity, making it easier for businesses to build application networks that connect their apps, data, and devices with APIs.

**Oracle NetSuite**: A comprehensive cloud-based business software suite, Oracle NetSuite offers tools for ERP, CRM, and e-commerce, enabling businesses to manage core processes in one integrated system.

**Pipe Technologies**: Revolutionizing SaaS financing, Pipe allows companies to turn recurring revenue into upfront capital, fostering sustainable growth without traditional loans.

**ProsperStack**: A churn reduction solution, ProsperStack assists subscription businesses in retaining customers through customizable offboarding flows and actionable feedback insights.

**Intuit QuickBooks Online**: A premier accounting software, Intuit Quickbooks assists businesses in managing finances, tracking expenses, and generating invoices, streamlining financial operations.

**Salesforce**: The world's #1 CRM platform, Salesforce empowers businesses with cloud-based solutions to connect with customers, streamline operations, and drive growth.

**Snowflake**: A cloud-based data warehousing company, Snowflake enables businesses to securely store, process, and analyze vast amounts of data with ease and flexibility.

**Tray.io**: A powerful automation platform, Tray.io allows businesses to integrate their entire software stack and automate complex processes, enhancing efficiency and productivity.

**Vertex Tax**: A leading provider of tax technology solutions, Vertex offers businesses comprehensive tax calculation and compliance tools, optimizing global tax management.

**Xero**: A cloud-based accounting software, Xero provides small to medium-sized businesses with tools for invoicing, bank reconciliation, and financial reporting, streamlining financial management.

**Zapier**: A robust automation tool, Zapier connects diverse apps to streamline workflows, enabling businesses to automate tasks and optimize productivity.

**Zendesk**: A leading customer service platform, Zendesk provides businesses with solutions to improve customer support, foster engagement, and streamline communication.
