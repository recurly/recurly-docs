# Overview

### Additional cost

This feature or setting requires an additional cost. Please reach out to your Recurly account manager or [support@recurly.com](mailto:support@recurly.com) for more pricing details.

### Prerequisites

- Familiarity with the third-party software you intend to integrate.
- An active account or subscription with the third-party service.
- API keys or authentication credentials, as required by the third-party software.

### Limitations

- Integration capabilities are dependent on the third-party software's API and feature updates.
- Some features may not be fully compatible or may require additional configuration.
- Regular maintenance and updates may be needed to ensure seamless integration.

# Definition

Partner Integrations in the realm of Accounting, Fraud, and Taxes refer to the collaboration and connection between our platform and third-party software solutions. These integrations allow users to combine the best features of both platforms, ensuring streamlined operations, accurate financial management, and robust fraud prevention.

# Key benefits

- **Efficiency:** Automate and streamline accounting, tax calculations, and fraud detection processes.
- **Accuracy:** Reduce human errors by automating data transfers and calculations.
- **Security:** Benefit from advanced fraud detection and prevention mechanisms.
- **Unified Dashboard:** Manage all integrations from a single platform, simplifying operations.
- **Scalability:** Easily add or remove integrations as your business needs evolve.

# Key details

**Avalara AvaTax Integration**

Integrate with [Avalara AvaTax](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/avalara) to automate sales tax calculations, ensuring compliance and accuracy in real-time.

**Avalara for Communications (AFC)**

Harness the power of [AFC](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/afc) to calculate taxes on telecom services, ensuring regulatory compliance across jurisdictions.

**Vertex Tax Integration**

Connect with [Vertex ](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/vertex)to automate and streamline your tax processes, from calculations to compliance, across multiple regions.

**Xero Integration**

Synchronize your financial data with [Xero](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/xero), a leading online accounting software, for real-time insights and efficient bookkeeping.

**QuickBooks Online Integration**

Integrate with [QuickBooks ](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/quickbooks)Online to manage your finances, invoices, and expenses seamlessly, all from one platform.

**Kount (Fraud) Integration**

Enhance your platform's security by integrating with [Kount](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/kount), a premier fraud detection and prevention solution.

**Oracle NetSuite Integration**

Connect with [Oracle NetSuite](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/recurly-for-oracle-netsuite) to manage your business finances, operations, and customer relations more effectively and efficiently.
