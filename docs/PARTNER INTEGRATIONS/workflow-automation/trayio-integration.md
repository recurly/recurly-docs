# Overview

# Required plan

This feature or setting is available to all customers on any Recurly subscription plan.

# Definition

Tray.io is an extensive integration platform that allows businesses to connect their various systems and automate complex processes. Through its intuitive interface and extensive connectivity options, Tray.io integrates seamlessly with Recurly to streamline subscription management and enhance data flow across your software ecosystem.

# Key benefits

- **Enhanced connectivity**: Connect your Recurly account with both cloud-based and on-premise systems across your business, customer, and partner ecosystems, maximizing operational efficiency.
- **Reliable data integration**: Maintain consistent data across your applications, even when native integrations fall short, ensuring accurate billing and subscription data in Recurly.
- **Customizable process automation**: Automate your subscription management and billing processes in Recurly according to your business needs, freeing up resources and reducing dependency on standard out-of-the-box solutions.

# Key details

- **Stack connectivity**: Tray.io offers extensive connectivity options, allowing you to integrate systems whether they are located in the cloud, on-premise, or across different environments of your ecosystem.
- **Data integration**: With Tray.io, you can ensure that your data remains synchronized across your evolving software landscape, providing a consistent and accurate view of your subscription metrics.
- **Process automation**: Break free from the limitations of standard tools with Tray.io’s capabilities to tailor automation in Recurly, optimizing workflows and enhancing efficiency.
- **Ecosystem activation**: Utilize Tray.io to activate and deliver ecosystem integrations that benefit not only your internal team but also your customers and partners.
- **API management**: Modernize your API management with Tray.io’s low-code and code-optional approaches. Speed up API integration and reduce development efforts with the support of Tray Merlin AI, enhancing your Recurly implementation.
