# Onboarding and migration

<div class="rp-definition">
  Getting started with Recurly should be smooth, not stressful. Our onboarding and migration team brings deep subscription and payments expertise to your implementation — handling everything from initial scoping to PCI-compliant data migration so you can focus on your business.
</div>

<div class="rp-steps">
  <div class="rp-step">
    <div class="rp-step-num">1</div>
    <div><h4>Initial consultation</h4><p>Our subscription and payments experts work closely with you to understand your business goals and guide you through the implementation process. You'll get practical, experience-backed guidance to drive success in subscription management from day one.</p></div>
  </div>
  <div class="rp-step">
    <div class="rp-step-num">2</div>
    <div><h4>Implementation consultation</h4><p>We collaborate with you to design a Recurly configuration that fits your specific business requirements — accelerating your time-to-value and keeping disruptions to a minimum.</p></div>
  </div>
  <div class="rp-step">
    <div class="rp-step-num">3</div>
    <div><h4>Integration consultation</h4><p>Our integrations team provides expert guidance on best practices to ensure your integrations are robust, secure, and optimized for your workflows. We're here to make sure everything connects the way it should.</p></div>
  </div>
  <div class="rp-step">
    <div class="rp-step-num">4</div>
    <div><h4>Data migration</h4><p>Our migration experts handle the PCI-compliant transfer of your subscriber data with a focus on data integrity and confidentiality. You get a clean, complete migration — no data loss, no disruptions.</p></div>
  </div>
</div>
