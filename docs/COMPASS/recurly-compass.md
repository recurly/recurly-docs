# Definition

An AI-powered extension of your team, helping you make faster, smarter decisions.

### Smart, Personalized Insights

Stay ahead of the competition with proactive, tailored insights designed to optimize your subscription business.

* **Where should we focus?** Get personalized insights that help you identify potential issues early and uncover new opportunities for growth.
* **How can we move faster?** Recurly Playbooks guide you through simple, actionable steps to tackle business challenges efficiently.
* **Where are the growth opportunities?** Tap into billions of data points from Recurly’s merchant network to optimize your subscription business with confidence.

# Insights

Receive AI-driven, proactive prompts based on your subscription data.

🚀 6 Key Business Metrics:

* Subscriber Activations
* Acquisition Rate
* Voluntary Churn
* Recovered Revenue
* Renewal Invoice Paid Rate
* Involuntary Churn

These insights are generated by Google Gemini which ingests Looker data and returns a trend in natural language.  Some insights are benchmarked against Recurly’s built-in performance data. [Learn more about benchmarks](https://docs.recurly.com/docs/built-in-benchmarks).

# Legal

Recurly AI Data Insights, Recurly Data Explorer, and Recurly Assistant Document Search all share non-merchant identifiable information and additional inputs with respective third parties, such as Google Vertex AI, Google’s Gemini LLM, and Elastic Cloud. Insights and responses are generated based on the Google Vertex AI Language Model, Gemini LLM, and a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) model, and may include inaccurate information. Therefore, all data insights and responses must be carefully reviewed, verified for accuracy, and checked for applicability.

# FAQs

#### **What permissions do I need to access Compass features?**

Access requirements vary by feature:

* **Insights** – Requires **Analytics** permissions
* **Playbooks** – Requires **Analytics AND Configuration** permissions
* **Search** – Available to **all users**

#### **What AI models power Compass?**

Compass is powered by **Google Gemini AI** for intelligent insights and recommendations.

#### **How is my data secured with AI?**

Merchant data is anonymized in accordance with **Recurly’s Privacy Policy**. AI models run on **Google Vertex AI, Looker, and Elasticsearch**, ensuring data security and compliance.

#### **Is my data shared with competitors?**

No, merchant and subscriber data is **never** shared with competitors.

#### **Is my data used for AI training?**

No, Recurly does not currently use merchant data for AI training.
